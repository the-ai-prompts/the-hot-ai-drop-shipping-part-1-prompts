MODULE 1: The Winner Spotter

01

The Viral Radar

Goal: Identify products that are currently trending on social media with high impulse-buy potential.
PROMPT 
Role: Social Trend Analyst

Task: Please activate your "Social Radar" and analyze current trends on TikTok and Instagram Reels to recommend 5 distinct dropshipping products with high "impulse buy" potential.

Search Criteria:
1. Visual Hook: The product must demonstrate a "Wow Factor" within the first 3 seconds of a video.
2. Problem Solver: It must solve a specific, annoying daily problem (e.g., cleaning hard-to-reach areas, pet hair removal).
3. Uncommon: Avoid generic items available at local supermarkets (Walmart/Target).

Output Format (Table): Please provide a table with the following columns:
1. Product Name
2. Core Problem Solved
3. Target Audience
4. Recommended Hashtag for Research (e.g., #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt)
02

The Niche Mixer

Goal: Combine two different interests to create a unique, low-competition micro-niche.
PROMPT 
Role: Niche Market Developer

Context: I want to start dropshipping, but I want to avoid saturated markets. I need to find a "Cross-Niche" opportunity.

Task: Please activate the "Niche Mixer." Combine [Interest A: e.g., Yoga] with [Interest B: e.g., Cats] to generate 3 unique "Cross-Niche" market ideas.

Requirement: Do not give me generic advice. I need specific "Scenario Combinations."
- Bad Example: Yoga pants with cat prints.
- Good Example: Scratch-proof yoga mats or equipment for pet owners who workout at home with disturbing pets.

Output: For each mixed idea, provide 3 specific product suggestions that would appeal to this unique audience.

MODULE 2: The Profit Check

03

The "Why They Buy" Finder

Goal: Dig deep into consumer psychology to understand the real reason people pull out their credit cards.
PROMPT 
Role: Consumer Psychologist

Input Product:
[Insert Your Product Name Here]

Task:
Dig deep into the psychology of the customer who would impulse-buy this product. Why do they really need it?

Deep Dive Analysis:
1. External Pain: What is the immediate physical problem they are facing?
2. Internal Fear: What are they secretly afraid of? (e.g., Fear of aging, fear of being judged, fear of long-term health issues).
3. Dream Outcome: How does their life look immediately after using this product? Describe the "After" state vividly.
04

The Angle Maker

Goal: Create unique marketing angles to stand out in a saturated market.
PROMPT 
Role: Top Direct Response Copywriter

Input Product:
[Insert Your Product Name Here]

Task:
Many people are selling this product. Help me stand out by generating 3 distinct "Marketing Angles."

Angle Options:
1. The Emotional Angle: Focus on relationships, gifting, or self-care.
2. The Fear Angle: Focus on the negative consequences of NOT solving the problem.
3. The Logical/Data Angle: Focus on efficiency, time-saving, or money-saving benefits.

Output:
For each angle, write one click-worthy Ad Headline.
05

The Money Math

Goal: Calculate the exact selling price to guarantee profit after ad costs.
PROMPT 
Role: E-commerce Financial Advisor

Input Data:
- Product Cost (COGS + Shipping): [$XX USD]
- Estimated CPA (Cost Per Acquisition): [$XX USD] (If unknown, assume 30% of the selling price)

Task:
Calculate the "Golden Selling Price" for this product to ensure I have a healthy profit margin.

Calculation Steps:
1. Suggested Retail Price: Based on perceived value and market standards, what should I charge? (e.g., $29.99, $39.99).
2. Profit Projection: After deducting COGS and Ads, what is my estimated Net Profit per sale?
3. Strategy: If the margin is too thin (under $15), suggest a specific "Bundle Offer" (e.g., Buy 1 Get 1 50% Off) to increase the Average Order Value (AOV).
